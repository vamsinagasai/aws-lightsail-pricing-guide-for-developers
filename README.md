## Why AWS Lightsail Pricing Is More Complicated Than It Looks

### The Base Plan Is Just the Beginning

Lightsail advertises flat monthly pricing. What they do not advertise upfront:

- **Snapshots** are billed separately. Every backup costs money.
- **Static IPs** look free but get charged the moment they are unattached from an instance.
- **Load balancers** are $18/month extra. Every single one.
- **Data transfer overages** are $0.09/GB. One traffic spike and your bill doubles.
- **Managed databases** are not included. They start at $15/month on top of everything else.
- **DNS management** has its own limits and potential overages.

### Scaling on Lightsail Is a Nightmare

- Upgrading a Lightsail plan requires downtime
- You cannot scale individual components independently
- Moving to EC2 when you outgrow Lightsail requires a full manual migration
- There is no autoscaling built in

### The Operational Burden Never Goes Away

- Deployments are still manual
- You manage your own CI/CD pipelines
- Monitoring requires third party tools
- Security updates are your responsibility
- When something breaks, you fix it
